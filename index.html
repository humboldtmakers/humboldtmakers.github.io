Next Meeting: Wednesday, August 21st, 2013 @ 6pm at The Link (1385 8th St, Arcata, CA)

Dear Makers,

We'll be getting together on Wednesday evening 8/21 at 6pm at The Link, 1385 8th Street in Arcata (the Greenway Partners building.)

Folks from a local outfit called Woodlab will be doing a presentation/demonstration, and those who have taken on the tasks of coming up with an initial kid-friendly project, locating a possible space, and exploring funding opportunities will be able to report what they have found. I know Peter and Nick have been putting some time in. (Unfortunately, the Jefferson School in Eureka is not yet ready for a tenant like the one we would be, but Peter will have suggestions, and the Greenway Partners building may be an option as well.)

If you have projects you'd like to propose for support from the group, please come prepared to make a quick pitch. If you have ideas you'd like to brainstorm, please see if you can get them down on a single sheet of paper, and perhaps bring ten or twenty copies.

This get-together will be pot-luck. The Link has a sink and microwave. In addition to your enthusiasm, please consider bringing some food to share, with ingredients labelled so vegetarians, vegans, etc... can partake without fear.

Mitch Trachtenberg

7/30/2013 Meeting Minutes

Location: Propelsion / The Link (1385 8th St, Arcata, CA)

Main Organizer: Lonny Grafman

Meeting Structure

  • Intro to Propelsion / The Link
  • Intro to 3D Printing / Arduino
  • People Introductions
    • Name
    • Experience
    • Hopes For This Meeting
  • Update from "The Co-Op Group"
  • Small Group Discussion
    • Purpose of the Group/Meeting
    • Meeting Structure
  • Next Steps
  • Tour

The meeting concluded with a nominal-voting-technique vote on future group meeting structure. The winning structure was:

  • Meet twice per month; Short (~15 min) Presentation; Open Q&A (~30 min); Tinker Time
